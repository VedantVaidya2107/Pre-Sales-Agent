Constraints & Assumptions
Constraints
  • The actual delivery date may change based on the timely response of various stakeholders.
  • All third-party integrations will depend on the technical capabilities & API support of those platforms.
  • Implementation will depend on the Zoho platform's current capabilities and limitations.
  • All Zoho Plans have limits on Records, Storage, Custom functions, and API calls. If limits are exceeded during data migration, Integration, or usage, the Xcut Technologies team will need to purchase additional add-ons.
  • The above scope is a basic initial understanding. Final scope may evolve over time as we discover the requirements in detail.
  • Clarity on the type of automation desired is required, as it needs to be managed within the scope and capabilities of CRM.
  • Reporting-hierarchy based data visibility will be used as the standard sharing model. If there are additional complex requirements, Zoho Analytics will be used.
  • Google Maps API key, usage limits, and billing will be managed under the client's Google account & need to be purchased separately by the Xcut Technologies team.
  • Mobile devices used by sales engineers will have GPS and internet access enabled for accurate geo-location capture.
  • Any additional reports, automations, or integrations beyond the agreed scope will be treated as change requests.
  • KM calculation is based on geo-location captured at visit check-in and check-out; continuous GPS tracking or route optimization is out of scope.
  • The accuracy of distance calculation depends on GPS accuracy, network availability, and Google Maps API responses.
Assumptions
  • The scope baseline will be finalized through Requirement Gathering & FSD sign-off, and the configuration will be implemented as per the approved FSD for Phase 1.
  • Data migration (if any) is assumed to be basic and limited unless separately specified. Detailed cleansing/deduplication beyond configured rules is not included.
  • SAP Business One is the system of record for all required sales/finance/inventory data used in Zoho Analytics reports.
  • Required SAP B1 data objects are accessible via API/DB/export, and the client will provide the required access and documentation.
  • Clean and validated master and transactional data will be provided from SAP Business One for integration and analytics.
