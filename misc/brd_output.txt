Part 1: Introduction and Project Vision (Pages 1–10)
Section                                                           Key Information and Highlights
Business Objectives                                                Improve lead-to-conversion rates, establish accountability, enable a unified Customer 360 view, and standardize processes across all products and
branches.
Products Covered                                                   Locker Services, Mutual Funds (Distributor), Financial Resource Products including Fixed Deposits (FD), Non-Convertible Debentures (NCD), and
Structured Deposits (SD).
Stakeholders & Roles                                             Relationship Managers (RM), Customer Development (CD) Staff, Branch Managers, Marketing Managers, and Product Managers.
As-Is Challenges                                                     Manual dependency on Excel sheets, physical registers, and WhatsApp resulting in poor accountability, duplicate follow-ups, lack of ROI/CAC visibility,
and inconsistent onboarding.
Part 2: Marketing, Pre-Sales, and Lead Management (Pages 11–57)
Section                                                           To-Be Vision & Capabilities
Marketing Automation                                             Automated drip campaigns, campaign approval workflows, and structured ROI tracking with all digital channels feeding directly into the CRM.
Lead Scoring                                                           Automated scoring framework based on demographics, product interest, and lead source to classify leads as Hot, Warm, or Cold.
Lead Routing & Assignment                                   Automatic routing using Pincode/Branch and product interest with round-robin or weight-based distribution logic.
Customer Profiling (CLV & RFM)                            Capture and leverage Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and RFM metrics for segmentation, personalization, and retention strategies.
Part 3: Sales, Onboarding, and Activity Management (Pages 58–80)
----------------Page (1) Break----------------
Section                                                           Sales & Activity Requirements
Sales Lifecycle                                                        Defined opportunity stages from S0 (Enquiry) to S4 (Loss/Discard) ensuring complete lifecycle visibility.
Activity Management                                               CRM-driven Permanent Journey Plans (PJP), daily task management, and geo-tagged field visit check-in/check-out.
Onboarding & QDE                                                 Standardized Quick Data Entry (QDE) with mandatory digital document uploads and configurable folder structures.
Part 4: Product-Specific Process Flows (Pages 81–140)
Product                                                          Key CRM Flow Requirements
Locker Services                                                      Integration with Locker Management System (LMS) to check availability, reserve lockers, and track agreement and booking status.
Financial Resources (FD, NCD, SD)                      Payment-stage tracking, interest payout frequency capture, and bank/PAN/Aadhaar validation during onboarding.
Mutual Funds Distribution                                       Integration with Mutual Fund platforms (MWB/Redvision) for KYC, folio creation, scheme selection, and SIP/Lumpsum execution.
Part 5: Service, Integration, and System Requirements (Pages 141–164)
Section                                                           Key System and Service Requirements
Service & Support (Zoho Desk)                              Centralized ticketing with automated assignment, SLA tracking, resolution monitoring, and structured query categorization.
Customer Portal (SFSL One)                                  Branded portal for customers and partners to track application status, upload documents, and view reports.
Integration Footprint                                                Locker Management System, Mutual Fund Apps, SFL Core System, and third-party e-Sign services.
System Security                                                      SAML/OAuth-based Single Sign-On (SSO) using corporate credentials.
Reporting (MIS)                                                       MIS dashboards covering sales productivity, marketing ROI, and service SLA adherence.
